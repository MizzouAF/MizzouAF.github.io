About Me


My name is Jared Hall. I grew up in Southwest Missouri, where the Ozarks meet the prairie and the weather gets very interesting. This developed a passion for the natural world, and for the last eighteen years I have served in the United States Air Force. Thirteen of those years, I have worked as a Meteorologist. However, most of my career has been in training and developing others. Mostly, I have been assigned the task of training and certifying junior Airmen for weather deployments around the globe.


This love of teaching and training is what has led me back to the University of Missouri. This is my first adventure into web development. I hope to be able to use this course to further a career in instructional design. In my spare time, I like to hike, hunt, kayak, and spend time with my girlfriend Kellie, my family, and my friends.


I have two associate degrees in Electronics Technology and Meteorology from the Community College of the Air Force. I also graduated Magna Cum Laude from Park University with a Bachelor of Science in Geography. Currently, I am a Learning Technologies and Design graduate major at the University of Missouri. My estimated graduation date is Spring 2024.
