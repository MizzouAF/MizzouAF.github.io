Decoding The Sky



Greetings! And Welcome to “Decoding the Sky” the hobby meteorologist’s training for how to understand and decode the weather!

We hope you enjoy the website and the journey of learning weather observation with us!


Have you ever found yourself fascinated by the weather? Do you constantly look toward the sky? Here at “Decoding the Sky” we are just like you! The purpose of this website is to help people like ourselves who are interested in weather. We would like to extend your understanding of the natural world, by understanding the changes within our atmosphere.


We hope that through the interaction on our website that you will be able to conduct your own meteorological observations at home. Our objective is to have at-home weather enthusiasts be able to evaluate the current weather situation occurring around them with the aid of their physical senses and some basic weather instruments.
